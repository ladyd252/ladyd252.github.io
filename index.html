ActiveRecord Lite

A functional replica of ActiveRecord, written in Ruby. Parses SQL queries to create objects from database and saves new object instances to the databas. Also enables creation of direct associations and through associations.

A remake of the classic arcade video game! A collaboration with Cody McNamara.
